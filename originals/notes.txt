ScummVM Brand Package 2009
==========================
by Jean Marc Gimenez

Contents:

- New ScummVM logo .psd
- New ScummVM logo .svg
- ScummVM icons .psd

Notes
-----
The font used in the ScummVM logo is the bold variant of
'Billy' by David Buck of New Zealand type foundry Sparkytype.

The tools badge is a slightly modified version of Andy's
"Tools, Hammer, Spanner" from openclipart.org
(http://openclipart.org/cchost/media/files/Andy/67)
